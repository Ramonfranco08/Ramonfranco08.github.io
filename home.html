My name is Ramon Franco, I started doing art since I was a toddler living in Mexico. My neverending passion to create, drives me every day to push the materials im working with to the limits and inventing new ways to bring good cuality to my work. I'm a spatial artist living in San Jose California. I’m currently a student and working on my craft in San Jose State University. My BFA is focused in Spatial Arts and sculpture. I have worked in multiple materials and I’m currently working on finishing my second solo exhibition. please check out my portfolio and my youtube page for more about my work.
